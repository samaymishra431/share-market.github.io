ONBOARDING
How do I create an account?
To create an account, download the FundMF app, click on "Sign Up," and follow the on-screen instructions to complete your registration.
What documents do I need for KYC?
You'll need a valid ID proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport), address proof, and a recent photograph for KYC verification.
How long does verification take?
KYC verification typically takes 1-2 business days, but may sometimes be completed in a few hours.
REGISTRATION
How do I complete my profile?
Go to Account > Profile and fill in all required fields including your personal information and bank details.
Can I register multiple bank accounts?
Yes, you can register up to 5 bank accounts in your FundMF profile for redemptions and investments.
TRANSACTION
What payment methods are accepted?
We accept Netbanking, UPI, NEFT/RTGS transfers, and auto-debit mandates for SIPs.
How long does redemption take?
Redemption typically takes 2-3 business days for equity funds and 1 business day for debt funds.
How do I set up an SIP?
Go to Invest > SIP, select your desired fund, frequency, and amount. Then set up a payment mandate to complete the process.
OTHERS
How do I track my investments?
Go to Dashboard or Portfolio to view all your investments, returns, and transaction history.
How to generate account statements?
Go to Portfolio > Statements and select the desired time period to generate and download your account statement.
SUPPORT
What are your support hours?
Our support team is available Monday to Saturday, 9 AM to 6 PM. For urgent issues, you can use our 24/7 chatbot.
How do I report an issue?
You can report issues through the Help section, by sending a message, or by calling our customer care number.
